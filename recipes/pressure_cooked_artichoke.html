Pressure Cooked Artichoke

Artichokes can be difficult to cook properly due to being so tough. Try pressure cooking to cook them quickly to perfection

Ingredients

  • 1 artichoke

Instructions

  1. Pour 1/2" of water in bottom of pressure cooker with vegtable steamer
  2. Cut artichoke in half and place in pressure cooker
  3. Seal pressure cooker and cook on high until pressure begins to release
  4. Continue to cook for 5 minutes
  5. Turn of burner and leave pressure cooker on burner until pressure safety lock releases
  6. Remove and serve
