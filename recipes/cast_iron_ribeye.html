Cast Iron Ribeye

Perfect Keto Dinner

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 1 ribeye, 1 lb each
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp canola oil

Instructions

  1. Season ribeye with salt and pepper on both sides
  2. Allow ribeye to sit for 30-60 minutes
  3. Pre-heat oven to 350 deg
  4. Heat cast iron with oil on high
  5. Place ribeye on cast iron once oil starts to smoke
  6. Sear for 2 minutes per side
  7. Place cast iron in oven for 6 minutes
  8. Remove from oven and allow to rest in pan for 10 minutes
