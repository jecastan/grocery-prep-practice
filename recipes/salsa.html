Salsa

A tasty and spicy topping!

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 6 tomatoes, roma
  • 6 serranos
  • 1 onion, purple, medium
  • 1 bunch of cilantro
  • 2 tbsp salt

Instructions

  1. Chop the stems off the serranos
  2. Combine tomatoes and serranos in a pot of water and boil, covered, for 30 minutes
  3. Allow pot to cool to room temperature
  4. Blend tomatoes and serranos in blender and add to a large bowl
  5. Add salt and mix well
  6. Dice onion and cilantro finely and stir into salsa
  7. Refridgerate immediately
