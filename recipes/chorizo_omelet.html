Chorizo Omelette

Tasty brunch

Servings

1

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 pack of chorizo
  • 2 oz of onion
  • 2 oz of mushrooms
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs in a bowl and add salt and pepper
  2. Dice onion and mushrooms
  3. Pre-heat skillet on medium heat and add chorizo
  4. Cook chorize for about 5-10 minutes
  5. Add onion and mushrooms and cook an additional few minutes
  6. Lower heat, add beat eggs and gently mix all ingredients together
  7. Cover the pan and cook until the mixture appears mostly solidified
  8. Remove cover and gently fold omelette in half
  9. Cook a minute or two more and then serve
